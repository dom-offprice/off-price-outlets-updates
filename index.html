Quick answers

Before you visit

Where is the warehouse?

We’re at 110 Republic Drive (left end unit), North Haven, CT 06473 — easy from New Haven, Hamden, Wallingford, and nearby towns.

When is the next sale?

Saturday & Sunday, August 1–2, 2026, from 10 AM to 4 PM both days. Follow us for future dates.

What deals can I expect?

Warehouse pricing on brand-name pieces — often up to 90% off retail. This sale: open-toe shoes $15, closed-toe shoes, dress shoes & boots $20, sneakers $25. Clothing: short & long-sleeve tops from $5, pants, shorts, swim, dresses, and men’s polos & dress shirts from $10, denim from $15. Women’s plus sizes and men’s up to 3XL.

Is this a regular retail store?

No — we’re an event-style warehouse outlet. We’re open during announced sale weekends only. That’s how prices stay this low.

How do I get sale reminders?

Follow @OffPriceOutletsCT on Instagram, @offpriceoutlets on TikTok, and Off Price Outlets on Facebook. Restocks, giveaways, and dates post there first.
